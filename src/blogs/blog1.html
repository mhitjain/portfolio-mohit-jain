üß† What is Chain-of-Thought Prompting?

Chain-of-Thought (CoT) prompting is a technique that instructs AI models to show their reasoning process step-by-step before arriving at a final answer‚Äîjust like Chef-Bot learned to do!

üí° The Key Insight

By forcing the model to articulate intermediate reasoning steps, CoT dramatically improves accuracy on complex tasks‚Äîturning "black box" AI into a transparent thinking partner.

Two Ways to Trigger CoT:

Zero-Shot CoT

Simply add a magic phrase:

Example Prompt Q: If I invest $5000 at 6% annual interest
   for 3 years, how much will I have?

No examples needed!

Few-Shot CoT

Provide examples with reasoning:

Example Prompt Q: Roger has 5 balls. He buys 2 cans
   with 3 balls each. How many total?

A: Roger started with 5 balls.
